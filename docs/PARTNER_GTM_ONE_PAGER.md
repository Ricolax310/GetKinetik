## What networks get

1. **Public read** — independent pass on their public endpoints (coordinates, cells, on-chain supply shape). Open methodology; anyone can rerun.
2. **Optional device check** — Proof of Origin URL → valid/invalid, chain age, score band. Their verifier still runs; we're a second read.
3. **No integration required** to *receive* a first report. Integration is the upsell when they want ongoing verify-device in payout logic.

We do **not** accuse fraud in outreach. We send **shape + grep lists** and ask who owns registry / treasury labels.
