HOW IT WORKS

One sovereign node. Many DePIN networks. One signed ledger.

DePIN earnings are scattered across half a dozen apps and chains. GETKINETIK aggregates them under a single hardware-signed identity — no custody, no token movement, only verifiable receipts.

1

MINT

A sovereign Ed25519 key is sealed inside your device.

On first activation, the app generates a hardware-backed Ed25519 keypair in your phone's secure enclave. The secret never leaves the device. The public key becomes your sovereign node ID — a permanent, Sybil-resistant identity that no company controls.

2

AGGREGATE

Earnings are read from every DePIN you run.

Plug in your wallet addresses. The app queries each DePIN network's public chain — Polygon, Solana, Arbitrum, Polkadot — and reads your token balance directly. We never custody funds. We never touch your tokens. We only observe and record.

3

SIGN

Every earning becomes a signed, hash-chained receipt.

Each delta-earning is recorded as a Ed25519-signed entry on a tamper-evident local ledger. The 1% protocol fee is baked into every signature so it's publicly auditable. Anyone can verify your earnings at getkinetik.app/verify with nothing but your public key.

THE MOAT

Anyone can build an aggregator. Nobody else can prove the nodes are real.

01

SEALED IN HARDWARE

The Ed25519 key is minted inside your device the first time you activate the node. It is sealed in silicon and cannot leave. No server holds it. No company holds it. You hold it. Every aggregator on the market routes through a custodial backend. We don't.

02

UPTIME IS MATHEMATICS

Every thirty seconds, your node signs a statement. Each statement is hash-chained to the one before. Bending one breaks them all. Helium bled revenue to fake hotspots. Hivemapper wrestles GPS spoofers. Our chain is the Sybil-resistance primitive every DePIN wishes they had.

03

PROOF IS THE ONLY COIN

Every earning aggregated in GETKINETIK traces back to an Ed25519 signature your device alone can produce. The 1% protocol fee is baked into the same signature, so the fee is publicly auditable forever. No off-chain accounting. No trust required.
