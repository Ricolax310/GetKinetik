HOW IT WORKS

Three steps. One tamper-proof bureau grade.

1

MINT

A sovereign Ed25519 key is sealed inside your device.

The private key is generated inside your phone's hardware secure enclave. It never leaves the device. The public key becomes your permanent, Sybil-resistant node ID.

2

PROVE

Every 30 seconds your node signs a tamper-evident heartbeat.

Each beat includes timestamp, device sensors, and battery state — hash-chained to the previous. Alter one beat and every subsequent signature breaks.

3

GRADE

The bureau scores your node 0–1000 and publishes the grade.

Partner networks can call our API when they want a trust sanity check before weighting verified-user premiums. The methodology is public. The score is yours forever.
