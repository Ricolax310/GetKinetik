HOW IT WORKS

One sovereign node. A hardware-sealed identity. A public bureau grade.

Every GETKINETIK node mints a cryptographic identity, signs a tamper-evident uptime record, and earns a public Genesis Score — the bureau's grade on whether the device is real. One call to the verify API returns it all.

1

MINT

A sovereign Ed25519 key is sealed inside your device.

On first activation, the app generates a hardware-backed Ed25519 keypair in your phone's secure enclave. The secret never leaves the device. The public key becomes your sovereign node ID — a permanent, Sybil-resistant identity that no company controls.

2

PROVE

Every 30 seconds your node signs a tamper-evident heartbeat.

While the node is live, it emits a signed statement: timestamp, device sensors (motion, pressure, light), and battery state. Each beat is hash-chained to the previous one. The chain makes uptime mathematically provable — alter one beat and every subsequent signature breaks.

3

GRADE

The bureau scores your node 0–1000 and publishes the grade.

GETKINETIK reads your signed proof, verifies the Ed25519 chain, and computes a Genesis Score — a public, non-transferable reputation grade. Partner networks call our API before they pay verified-user premiums. The methodology is public; the score is yours forever.

Every network adapter is a standalone TypeScript package (@getkinetik/adapter-*) that feeds the same sovereign signing and verification pipeline. The bureau grades nodes for each network independently — one bad actor on one network doesn't contaminate another.

HONEST ON CONTINUITY

Your Genesis seniority lives on this phone.

Your bureau grade and heartbeat seniority are bound to the hardware-sealed identity on this device. Your tokens on Nodle, DIMO, Hivemapper, and the rest stay in your wallets on those networks; we never held them. Seed-phrase backup and restore is now live in the app — open your Proof of Origin card, tap Reveal Recovery Phrase, and write down your 12 words. That phrase lets you move your node to a new phone without losing seniority. One active device at a time; the same guarantee as every hardware wallet you already trust.

What happens if I lose this phone?

Partner-network earnings remain yours wherever those wallets live. Your Genesis Score and seniority are tied to this node ID on this device — lose the device without a backup and the bureau sees a new identity with a fresh clock. That is the trade for Sybil-resistant, hardware-rooted proof today.

How do I back up or restore my node?

Live now on Android. Open the Proof of Origin card (tap the ruby PROOF chip on the main screen), scroll to Recovery Phrase, and tap to reveal your 12 BIP-39 words. Write them down offline. To restore on a new device, tap Restore from Seed Phrase on the lock screen, enter your 12 words, and your node ID and seniority are back instantly — one active device at a time.

Why is there no cloud login?

We do not want custody of your keys or a server-side copy of your identity. The verifier and bureau APIs are public; the secret stays on-device so the signature chain stays meaningful.
