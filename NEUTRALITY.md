# NEUTRALITY CHARTER

> **GETKINETIK is the independent trust layer for the decentralized
> physical economy. This document is the immutable contract that makes
> our grading worth trusting. Drift from these rules ends the
> company's reason to exist.**

---

## Why this document exists

Credit bureaus, rating agencies, financial data utilities, and reputation
infrastructure providers — Carfax, Equifax, S&P, Moody's, Bloomberg,
Stripe Radar, Plaid, Etherscan — share one structural property: they
have nothing to gain from any specific outcome in the markets they
grade. The moment they do, the grade becomes worthless.

DePIN currently has no such neutral entity. Every network grades
itself. Every foundation publishes its own KPIs. No one independently
attests to whether a node is real, an operator is honest, or earnings
are accurate.

GETKINETIK fills that slot — but only if it remains structurally
incapable of corruption. This document defines those structural rules.
Future operators of the company are bound by them.

---

## The Five Rules (immutable)

These are the rules that protect the bureau. They are not subject to
quarterly business pressure, partner requests, capital incentives, or
strategic exceptions.

### 1. **No GETKINETIK token. Ever.**

We will not issue a token, coin, point, NFT, security, or any other
fungible or non-fungible instrument that is sold, traded, swapped,
listed on an exchange, redeemable for currency, or capable of
appreciating in market value.

This includes "utility tokens," "governance tokens," "loyalty tokens,"
"reward tokens," "soulbound tokens," wrapped representations of
internal scores, or any future financial wrapper that has the
economic shape of a token regardless of the legal label applied to it.

**Genesis Score is a public reputation grade about a node, not a
currency.** It is non-transferable, non-priced, never redeemable, and
cannot be bought, sold, traded, or held by anyone other than the node
it grades.

### 2. **No equity in graded networks.**

GETKINETIK and OutFromNothing LLC will not hold equity, tokens, vested
allocations, advisor shares, or any economic interest in any DePIN
network whose nodes the bureau grades.

We will not accept payment in the native token of a graded network
(NODL, $DIMO, HONEY, WXM, GEOD, etc.). All revenue flows in fiat
(USD) or chain-neutral stablecoin (USDC/USDT). If a partner cannot pay
in those denominations, they cannot pay.

### 3. **No exclusive partnerships.**

Every DePIN network is graded by the same published methodology. We
do not soften the grade for any partner, regardless of how much they
pay or how strategic they are. We do not offer "preferred network"
status, "promoted listings," or any commercial product that distorts
the underlying grading.

Partners pay for **API access**, **integration support**, and **SLA
tiers** — never for favorable treatment of their network in the data.

### 4. **All revenue in fiat or stablecoin.**

The complete revenue model:

| Tier | Pays | Receives |
|------|--------|------------|
| **Free** | Anyone | Public verifier, basic ledger, own data export |
| **Consumer Pro** | Individual users (USD subscription) | Advanced reports, hardware-ROI calculators, exportable history |
| **Partner API** | DePIN networks, lenders, insurers, exchanges (USD/USDC, monthly billed) | `verify-device` calls, batch attestation, fraud signals, compliance reports |
| **Enterprise** | Foundations, auditors, governments, large platforms (USD, contracted) | Full read API access, methodology disclosure, custom attestation classes, SOC 2 reports |

Money in: fiat or stablecoin only. Money out: company operating
expenses. No token treasury. No vested founder coins. No "ecosystem
fund" we control. The P&L statement fits on one page.

### 5. **Methodology is public.**

The Genesis Score formula, the verify-device validation rules, the
attestation index methodology, and any future grading systems are
**publicly documented and versioned** — like FICO publishes its score
components, not the proprietary weights but the inputs and their
direction.

Any methodology change is announced **before** it takes effect, with
a public comment period when the change materially affects scores.
Old scores remain queryable so partners can audit historical
decisions.

We publish a **transparency report** at least annually covering: total
nodes graded, total partner queries served, methodology updates made,
disputes received and resolved, and any pressure attempts from
networks that we declined.

## What we will do

- **Charge fairly for API access** in fiat/stablecoin to partner
  networks, lenders, insurers, exchanges, and any other party that
  needs neutral attestation.
- **Publish methodology openly** so anyone can audit our audits.
- **Publish quarterly conflict-of-interest disclosures** listing every
  partner we have a commercial relationship with and the nature of it.
- **Refund or refuse business** that requires us to soften grading.
- **Defend the bureau in writing** when networks pressure us — and
  publish the pressure when appropriate.
- **Open-source the verifier** (already done — see `landing/verify/`)
  so anyone can independently confirm our cryptographic claims.

## What we will not do

- Issue any token, coin, point system with monetary value, or financial
  wrapper.
- Hold equity, tokens, or economic interest in any graded network.
- Accept payment in graded networks' native tokens.
- Promise specific score outcomes to partners in exchange for
  payment, integration, or access.
- Offer "promoted" placements, "preferred" listings, or any commercial
  distortion of the underlying grading.
- Sell user data. The bureau publishes grades, not user-level
  identifiable information.
- Operate as a financial services provider, custodian, exchange,
  market maker, or anything that creates regulatory entanglement with
  the networks we grade.

## Amendment process

**Rules 1–5 are not amendable** without dissolving the company and
starting a separate legal entity that is no longer presented as
GETKINETIK or as a neutral trust layer for DePIN.

Lower-level operational policies (specific pricing, specific
partner-tier names, specific methodology weightings) may evolve and
will be versioned in this repository under
[`docs/methodology/`](./docs/) when published. Material changes
require public notice in advance.

## Why this document is the company

The day the founder is offered a $50M token grant in exchange for
softening Genesis Score weighting on a partner network, this document
exists so the answer is **no** without a meeting. The day a foundation
offers GETKINETIK an exclusive verification contract for their
ecosystem, this document exists so the answer is **no, but every
network in the ecosystem can be a paid customer** without negotiation.
The day an acquirer wants to buy GETKINETIK to issue a token from the
data we hold, this document exists so the deal either restructures
around fiat operations or doesn't happen.

This document is the asset. The technology is the demo. The bureau
slot only stays open as long as we are the people other parties can
trust *not* to take the easy money.

We are deliberately structured to never have skin in the things we
grade. That is the entire moat. Everything else is implementation.
